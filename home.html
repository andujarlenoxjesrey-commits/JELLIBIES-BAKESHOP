HISTORY OF JELLIBIES BAKESHOP
Jellibies Bakeshop was founded on July 27, 2022.
It all started with the husband of Sol Anoba Debarra, who shared a small idea with her about opening a bakery.
They wanted to create a place where people could enjoy fresh and tasty baked goods.
At first, it was just a small shop with big dreams.
It all started in Oslob, the first branch. Their passion and hard work quickly attracted customers from the community.
Word spread about their delicious breads, cakes, and pastries.
Because of this support, the bakeshop began to grow.
Today, Jellibies Bakeshop has five branches serving happy customers.
It continues to focus on quality, taste, and friendly service.
From a simple idea to a thriving bakery, Jellibies Bakeshop has become a beloved part of the community.
LIFE OF THE FOUNDER
Sol Anoba Debarra was born on June 27, 1997, in Guiwang, Alcoy, and is now 28 years old. She was not able to pursue college, but she started working at Arbees Bakeshop at a young age as a collector. It was in Arbees where she met her future husband, who worked as a skilled baker.

As they worked together, they eventually shared one simple question that sparked everything: "Why not start our own bakeshop?" and thats the beginning of all
SUCCESS STORY OF JELLIBIES BAKESHOP
The journey of Jellibies Bakeshop grew from a simple idea into a thriving business. After working at Arbees Bakeshop, Sol and her husband learned important lessons about bakery operations, customer service, and the value of hard work. Their experience in Arbees became the foundation that guided them toward building their own brand.

Sol’s sisters, Lenicita Andujar and Lolita Paraqueles, believed in the vision and decided to invest. With their support and financial contribution, the business gained the strength it needed to start.

Through teamwork, dedication, and the skills learned from Arbees, the small dream grew into a successful and expanding bakeshop. Today, Sol, her husband, and her sisters proudly stand together as co-partners, managing Jellibies Bakeshop as a corporation with multiple branches serving the community.
CHALLENGES FACED BY THE FOUNDER/OWNER
Starting Jellibies Bakeshop was not easy. One of the first challenges Sol faced was finding the right baking equipment, which required a large amount of money to purchase. As a beginner in business, gathering complete tools and machines became a major obstacle.

Another struggle was the cost of rental for their first space. The rent was expensive, and managing the monthly payment became a heavy burden during the early stages of the business.

Despite these difficulties, Sol did not give up. With the help and support of her sisters—who contributed financially and believed in her dream—the business was able to push through. Their teamwork and determination allowed them to overcome the challenges and continue growing Jellibies Bakeshop into what it is today.
JELLIBIES BAKESHOP IN 10 YEARS
Ten years from now, Jellibies Bakeshop hopes to become a bigger and more trusted bakeshop that serves more people in different places. The business plans to open more branches while keeping the same good quality and service.

Jellibies Bakeshop also wants to provide more jobs and help more families by giving stable work opportunities. The goal is to continue growing while staying simple, honest, and customer-focused.

With teamwork, hard work, and faith, Jellibies Bakeshop aims to remain strong and successful even after ten years.

HOW THE PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT HELPED THE BUSINESS GROW
The Philippine government helped Jellibies Bakeshop grow by allowing it to operate as a legal and registered business. The bakeshop registered with the Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR), which made the business official and trusted by customers and suppliers.

Other important permits were also secured, such as the Barangay Clearance, Mayor’s Permit, and Sanitary Permit. These permits ensured that the business followed local rules, maintained cleanliness, and provided safe food for customers.

With these government requirements and guidance, Jellibies Bakeshop was able to operate smoothly, gain customer trust, and continue growing as a responsible local business.
